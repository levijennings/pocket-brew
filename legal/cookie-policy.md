# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies and similar technologies Pocket Brew (operated by DVLMNT) uses, and how you can manage your preferences.

## 1. What Are Cookies and Mobile Identifiers?

**Cookies** are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your browsing activity.

**Mobile Advertising Identifiers** are unique identifiers assigned to your mobile device that allow advertisers and analytics providers to track your activity across apps and websites. These include:
- IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) on iOS
- Android Advertising ID on Android devices

## 2. Types of Cookies and Identifiers We Use

### 2.1 Essential/Functional Cookies
These are required for Pocket Brew to function properly and include:
- Session management and user authentication
- Security tokens to prevent unauthorized access
- User preferences (like app theme or notification settings)
- Beer tracking data and local app storage

**These cannot be disabled without affecting core functionality.**

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies and Identifiers
These help us understand how users interact with Pocket Brew:
- **Firebase Analytics:** Tracks app usage patterns, feature usage, and crash data
- **PostHog:** Analyzes user behavior and feature adoption
- **Sentry:** Collects error and crash information

These analytics help us improve the Service and identify technical issues.

### 2.3 Advertising Identifiers
If you see personalized advertisements, we may use your device's advertising identifier to:
- Deliver relevant ads based on your interests
- Measure ad effectiveness
- Prevent ad fraud

We do not collect or transmit your advertising identifier to unaffiliated third parties without your consent.

### 2.4 Functional Identifiers
These help the app function better by remembering:
- Your search history and filters
- Saved breweries and venues
- Notification preferences
- Map zoom level and location preferences

## 4. How to Manage Cookies and Identifiers

### 4.1 App Settings
Within Pocket Brew, you can manage:
- Analytics collection (Settings > Privacy > Analytics)
- Location tracking (Settings > Privacy > Location)
- Notification preferences
- App theme and preferences

### 4.2 Device Settings

**iOS (IDFA Management):**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising
2. Tap "Personalized Ads" to toggle off
3. Tap "Reset Advertising ID" to get a new identifier

**Android (Advertising ID Management):**
1. Go to Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy
2. Scroll to "Ad settings"
3. Toggle "Personalized advertising" off (optional)
4. Tap "Reset advertising ID" to get a new identifier

### 4.3 App Uninstall
Uninstalling Pocket Brew will delete local app data and cookies from your device, though analytics and advertising data may persist with analytics and advertising providers.

## 5. Do Not Track

Some devices include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting. Pocket Brew respects device-level DNT preferences by limiting analytics collection when enabled.

## 6. What Happens if You Disable Cookies or Identifiers?

If you disable essential cookies, Pocket Brew may not function properly, including:
- Loss of login functionality
- Inability to save beer tracking data
- Loss of app preferences

Disabling analytics or advertising identifiers will not significantly affect functionality but may prevent us from improving the Service based on usage data.

## 7. Third-Party Analytics and Advertising

Pocket Brew uses third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services may collect data directly from your device and have their own privacy policies. By using Pocket Brew, you consent to data collection by these third parties as described in their privacy policies.
