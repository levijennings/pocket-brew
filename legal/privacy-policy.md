# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

DVLMNT ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Pocket Brew mobile application and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Pocket Brew, you consent to our data practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, age (to verify legal drinking age), and optional profile information.

**Beer Tracking Data:** We collect information about beers you track, including:
- Beer names, breweries, and styles
- Your ratings and reviews
- Tasting notes and preferences
- Dates and times of tastings
- Cost or price information
- Food pairings and personal preferences

**Location Data:** When you enable location services, we collect:
- Your precise geographic location
- Locations where you tasted specific beers
- Nearby breweries, bars, and liquor stores you visit
- Location history over time

**Social Sharing:** If you share tracking data or reviews on social media, we may collect information about your sharing activity.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including:
- Features used
- Beers searched or filtered
- Time spent in the app
- Actions taken (ratings, notes, check-ins)
- Crash reports and errors

**Device Information:** We collect technical information about your device:
- Device type, model, and operating system
- Device identifier (IDFA or Android Advertising ID)
- App version
- IP address
- Carrier information
- Whether location services are enabled

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies, mobile identifiers, and similar technologies to track usage and remember preferences.

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Sources

We may receive information from:
- Analytics providers
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms (if you use social login)
- Payment processors (for premium features)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 **Service Delivery:** We use your information to provide and maintain Pocket Brew, including:
- Processing and storing your beer tracking data
- Displaying your tasting notes and ratings
- Providing location-based features
- Enabling search and discovery

2.2 **Location Services:** We use location data to:
- Identify nearby breweries, bars, and liquor stores
- Show you locations where other users have tasted beers
- Provide location-based recommendations
- Improve location accuracy and coverage

2.3 **Communication:** We use your email to send:
- Service-related notifications (new features, updates)
- Responses to your inquiries
- Optional newsletters and promotional content (with opt-out capability)

2.4 **Safety and Compliance:** We use information to:
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Enforce our Terms and policies
- Comply with legal obligations
- Verify legal drinking age

2.5 **Analytics and Improvement:** We analyze usage patterns to understand user behavior and improve the Service.

2.6 **Personalization:** We personalize your experience through:
- Custom beer recommendations
- Curated feeds based on your preferences
- Suggested breweries and venues based on your location
- Personalized notifications

2.7 **Marketing:** With your consent, we may use your information for:
- Promotional emails about new features
- In-app advertisements
- Marketing partnerships (with opt-out capability)

## 3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 **Public Content:** Information you choose to make public, including tasting notes and ratings, is visible to other Pocket Brew users.

3.2 **Private Data:** Information you mark as private is not shared with other users unless you explicitly share it.

3.3 **Service Providers:** We share information with service providers who perform functions on our behalf:
- Supabase (database hosting and authentication)
- Mobile app analytics providers
- Payment processors (for premium features)
- Cloud storage providers
- Email delivery services

Service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

3.4 **Third-Party Services:**
- **Firebase Analytics:** Usage analytics
- **Sentry:** Error tracking and crash reporting
- **PostHog:** Feature usage and product analytics
- **Mapbox:** Location and mapping services

3.5 **Advertising Networks:** If you see personalized advertisements, we may share anonymized or hashed information with advertising networks.

3.6 **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or legal process, or to protect our rights and the safety of users.

3.7 **Business Transfers:** If DVLMNT is acquired or merges, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.8 **No Sale:** We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 **Storage Location:** Your data is stored on servers managed by Supabase, which may be located in various jurisdictions, including the United States.

4.2 **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard security, including:
- Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Secure password hashing

However, no security is 100% effective.

4.3 **Data Retention:** We retain your data while your account is active. Upon account deletion:
- Your tasting notes and ratings are deleted within 30 days
- Your location history is deleted within 30 days
- Account information and device identifiers may be retained longer for legal compliance
- Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to access your personal information and receive it in a portable format. To request this, email levi@dvlmnt.com with "Data Access Request" in the subject line.

### 5.2 Correction and Deletion
You have the right to correct inaccurate information and request deletion of your account and data. We will comply with deletion requests within 30 days (subject to legal retention requirements).

### 5.3 Opt-Out
You may opt out of:
- Marketing emails: Click unsubscribe link or manage preferences in account settings
- Location tracking: Disable location services in device settings
- Analytics: Disable analytics in app settings (though this may affect functionality)
- Behavioral advertising: Adjust device-level advertising preferences

### 5.4 CCPA Rights (California Residents)
If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Delete personal information (subject to exceptions)
- Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell information)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise CCPA rights, contact levi@dvlmnt.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

### 5.5 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
If you are an EU resident, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:
- Access to your personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise GDPR rights, contact levi@dvlmnt.com.

## 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

6.1 Pocket Brew is not intended for anyone under 21 years of age (or the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from users under the legal drinking age.

6.2 If we learn that we have collected information from someone under the legal drinking age, we will delete it promptly and may report the matter to law enforcement.

## 7. Location Privacy

7.1 **Precise Location:** Pocket Brew collects your precise location when enabled. This location data is sensitive and used only for the purposes described in this policy.

7.2 **Location History:** We retain your location history for up to 1 year to show you where you've tasted beers over time. You can delete location history from app settings.

7.3 **Sharing Locations:** When you publicly share a tasting note, it includes the location where you tasted the beer. You can edit this before sharing or mark notes as private.

7.4 **Device Permissions:** Your device controls location access. You can disable location services at any time in your device settings.

## 8. Third-Party Services

8.1 **Analytics:** We use Firebase, PostHog, and Sentry to understand app usage and track errors. These services may collect data directly from your device.

8.2 **Maps:** We use Mapbox to provide location features. See Mapbox's privacy policy: https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy

8.3 **Authentication:** If you use social login, we receive information from those providers in accordance with their privacy policies.

8.4 **Links to Third Parties:** The app may contain links to external websites and services not operated by DVLMNT. We are not responsible for their practices.

## 9. Cookies and Identifiers

9.1 **Mobile Identifiers:** The app uses mobile advertising identifiers (IDFA, Android Advertising ID) for analytics and personalized advertising.

9.2 **How to Manage:** You can reset or disable advertising identifiers in your device settings.

## 10. Data Transfers

If you are accessing Pocket Brew from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.
