# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the prohibited activities and conduct standards for users of Pocket Brew (operated by DVLMNT). By using Pocket Brew, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 1. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use Pocket Brew in any manner that:

### 1.1 Illegal Activities
- Violates any applicable federal, state, local, or international law or regulation
- Is used by individuals under the legal drinking age
- Violates laws regarding alcohol consumption or distribution
- Involves fraud, embezzlement, or financial crimes
- Constitutes harassment, stalking, or abuse
- Involves threats or violence
- Infringes intellectual property rights
- Violates privacy or data protection laws

### 1.2 System Interference
- Attempts to gain unauthorized access to Pocket Brew or its systems
- Disrupts the normal operation of the Service
- Overloads servers with excessive requests (DDoS attacks)
- Exploits vulnerabilities or bugs
- Interferes with network connectivity or functionality
- Transmits malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Uses automated tools to scrape or harvest user data without authorization

### 1.3 Fraudulent or Deceptive Conduct
- Misrepresents your identity or age
- Creates fake or impersonated accounts
- Engages in phishing or social engineering
- Manipulates ratings or reviews artificially
- Promotes fraudulent schemes or scams
- Falsifies location data to gain unauthorized access to location-restricted features

### 1.4 Spam and Abuse
- Posts repetitive, irrelevant, or off-topic content
- Sends unsolicited commercial messages to other users
- Exploits the messaging or notification system
- Creates content designed to manipulate search or recommendation algorithms
- Engages in coordinated inauthentic behavior to artificially inflate engagement

### 1.5 Harmful Content
- Posts content containing hate speech, discrimination, or racism
- Promotes violence, self-harm, or illegal activities
- Shares sexually explicit or obscene content
- Discloses other users' private information without consent
- Posts defamatory, libelous, or false statements about others
- Harasses, bullies, or threatens other users

### 1.6 Intellectual Property Violations
- Uploads or shares copyrighted content without authorization
- Violates trademark rights of others
- Shares trade secrets or confidential information
- Uses other users' content without attribution or permission

## 2. Product-Specific Rules

### 2.1 Beer Tracking and Reviews

You agree to:
- Provide honest ratings and reviews based on actual experience
- Focus reviews on the beer's flavor, quality, and characteristics
- Be respectful and constructive in feedback
- Only review beers you have actually tasted

You agree not to:
- Post fake reviews (as a brewery to promote your beer, or as a competitor to harm another)
- Include promotional links or solicitations in reviews
- Post multiple reviews of the same beer artificially
- Manipulate ratings through coordinated fake reviews or accounts
- Include personal contact information or external links in reviews
- Use reviews to promote unrelated products or services
- Post misleading information about beer styles, ingredients, or origins
- Share proprietary beer recipes or trade secrets in reviews

### 2.2 Location Data Usage

You agree to:
- Use location features only for legitimate beer tracking and discovery purposes
- Respect others' privacy regarding shared location information
- Not use location data to stalk, harass, or locate other users

You agree not to:
- Falsify location data to appear in different geographic areas
- Use location features to enable stalking or harassment of other users
- Share another user's location information without consent
- Collect location data about other users without authorization
- Use location data for competitive or commercial purposes

### 2.3 Tasting Notes and Content

You agree to:
- Not include harmful, illegal, or explicit content in tasting notes
- Not use tasting notes to promote illegal substances or activities
- Respect the intellectual property of breweries and creators
- Not impersonate breweries, beer reviewers, or other public figures in notes

You agree not to:
- Include health claims or medical advice in tasting notes
- Encourage excessive or dangerous alcohol consumption
- Use tasting notes to defame or disparage individuals or brands
- Share confidential information about breweries or events

### 2.4 Account Behavior

You agree to:
- Maintain only one account per person
- Use your account only for legitimate beer tracking
- Respect the accounts and content of other users
- Report violations to levi@dvlmnt.com

You agree not to:
- Create multiple accounts to manipulate rankings or circumvent restrictions
- Share account credentials with others
- Use another person's account without authorization
- Attempt to access other users' accounts or private data
- Buy, sell, or trade accounts

## 3. Content Standards

All user-generated content, including reviews, tasting notes, and ratings, must:

3.1 **Be Honest:** Content must not contain false, misleading, or deceptive information.

3.2 **Be Respectful:** Content must not demean, harass, or attack individuals or organizations.

3.3 **Be Appropriate:** Content must comply with laws and not promote illegal activities.

3.4 **Protect Privacy:** Content must not disclose private information about individuals without consent.

3.5 **Respect IP:** Content must not infringe the intellectual property rights of others.

3.6 **No Health Claims:** Content must not include medical, health, or dietary claims or advice.

## 4. Enforcement and Consequences

### 4.1 Monitoring
DVLMNT monitors user activity and content for compliance with this AUP. We may use automated tools and manual review to detect violations.

### 4.2 Content Removal
We may remove or suspend content that violates this AUP, including:
- False or manipulated reviews
- Violative tasting notes or comments
- Spam or repetitive content
- Content promoting illegal activities
- Explicit or harmful content

Content removal will occur without prior notice when the violation is clear.

### 4.3 Account Warnings
For minor violations, we may issue a warning describing the violation and requesting corrective action within a specified timeframe.

### 4.4 Account Suspension
For serious or repeated violations, we may temporarily suspend your account, restricting your ability to:
- Post reviews or tasting notes
- Create new entries
- Access certain features
- Interact with other users

### 4.5 Account Termination
We may permanently terminate your account if you:
- Repeatedly violate this AUP despite warnings
- Commit serious violations (fraud, illegal activity, severe harassment)
- Attempt to circumvent suspension restrictions
- Pose a threat to the Service or other users

### 4.6 Legal Action
For serious violations including illegal activity, we may pursue legal action and cooperate with law enforcement.

## 5. Reporting Violations

5.1 If you observe a violation of this AUP, please report it by:
- Using the in-app report feature on specific content
- Emailing levi@dvlmnt.com with "AUP Violation Report" in the subject line

5.2 Your report should include:
- Description of the violation
- Reference or link to the violating content
- Your contact information
- Any supporting evidence

5.3 We will investigate reports promptly and take appropriate action.

5.4 False or bad-faith reports may result in warnings or account suspension.

## 6. Appeals

6.1 If your account has been suspended or terminated, you may appeal within 30 days by emailing levi@dvlmnt.com with "Account Appeal" in the subject line.

6.2 Your appeal should include:
- Explanation of why you believe the action was inappropriate
- Evidence supporting your position
- Commitment to future compliance

6.3 We will review appeals within 14 days and notify you of the outcome.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

DVLMNT reserves the right to modify this AUP at any time. Changes are effective upon posting in the app. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of modifications.

## 8. Miscellaneous

8.1 **Compliance with Laws:** Regardless of this AUP, you agree to comply with all applicable laws, including those regarding alcohol consumption and distribution.

8.2 **Severability:** If any provision is found unenforceable, remaining provisions continue in effect.

8.3 **No Waiver:** Failure to enforce this AUP in specific instances does not constitute a waiver of DVLMNT's rights.
